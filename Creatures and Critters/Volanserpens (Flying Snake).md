Climate: Rainforest

Domain: Multicellula
Kingdom: Spiriare
Phylum: Terraspiriare
Class: Squama
Order: Periculumfunis
Family: Volanserpens

Diet: Carnivore, they'll eat nearly anything they can swallow whole or in large chunks. 

Description: Volanserpens are apex hunters of the rainforest. They are ambush predators that build nests in the canopy of the rainforest. Using the branches and vines to navigate the upper layers before diving down on prey. Some volanserpens kill their prey with venom and toxins, others target flying prey and slam them into the ground, killing them with the impact.
